# Instance Limits

Your account can run a set number of apps at once. New accounts start with a low
limit, and it rises on its own as your account establishes itself:

- Add a valid, verified payment method. An account without one runs very few
  apps.
- Let the account age. The limit is lower for the first month, then increases.

Established accounts in good standing reach the standard limit. If you need more
than that, contact support. When you hit the limit, the launch screen tells you:
"Please contact support to get your account limit increased."
